Section 1

Services Provided

LitterLuxe performs on-site cleaning, disassembly, sanitization, and reassembly of the Customer's Litter-Robot unit at the Customer's home. Services may include:

  • Removal of existing litter and waste
  • Enzyme treatment of all surfaces (globe, base, drawer, sensors)
  • Wet-vac extraction of dust, debris, and rinse water
  • Hospital-grade disinfection of unit components
  • Replacement of the carbon filter (included)
  • Exterior wipe-down and vacuuming of the surrounding mat area
  • Reassembly and a test cycle to confirm function

Specific services depend on the plan you selected (Reset Clean, Quarterly, or Monthly).

  • The Litter-Robot is an electronic appliance with components subject to normal wear. Pre-existing conditions, manufacturer defects, or component failure unrelated to the cleaning may become apparent during or after service.

Before any cleaning begins, the Service Provider will photograph the Litter-Robot and the immediate surrounding area to document existing condition. Why this matters: Pre-service photos protect both parties. They prevent disputes about damage that existed before we arrived, and they give you peace of mind that we're not responsible for issues we didn't cause.
